Skyhold: The Exile Engine

Utopia Above. Rebellion Below. The Engine Never Stops.

Skyhold: The Exile Engine is the first book in a sweeping sci-fi trilogy set in a floating city suspended above a broken world. Built on peace, powered by hidden truth, Skyhold is home to the elite — the exiled, the engineered, and the chosen.

Aari Vellin, a descendant of the architects who forged the city, discovers the existence of an ancient machine buried deep within Skyhold’s foundations — a machine that runs on memory, sacrifice, and myth. And it’s starting to fail.

As the city's perfect systems begin to fracture, a rogue captain, an exile with a secret, a shattered priestess, a cursed soldier, and a child with no past must uncover the lie that built the city — before it burns down in silence from the sky.

Skyhold is epic, layered, and cinematic — the beginning of a story about control, consciousness, and what we choose to remember.
