Pyramid Shift

One Pyramid. Two Realities. Infinite Consequences.

Pyramid Shift is a grungy, 90s-inspired sci-fi mystery where reality tears sideways. It begins with a viral TikTok video — a kid meditating with a glowing crystal pyramid on his chest. Weird but harmless… until someone tries it for real.

When the protagonist finds one of these pyramids at a sketchy antique shop, everything fractures. One minute he’s in his bedroom — the next, he’s in Creedland: a surreal mirror world built from comic books, buried guilt, and half-finished dreams. And time moves differently there.

As the line between both worlds decays, he must uncover why the pyramid chose him… and what it’s trying to take.

Pyramid Shift is a layered journey into memory, choice, and the space between dimensions — dressed in flannel, static, and the music of another life.
