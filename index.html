Meet Tomothy Morrow

Tomothy Morrow is a writer of dreams, digital echoes, and time-twisting tales. From dusty typewriters to blinking cursors, his stories navigate the liminal spaces between what is and what might be. When he’s not writing, he’s probably thinking about writing—or rewriting reality in his mind.
