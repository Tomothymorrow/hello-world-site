The First Five Minutes

A Darkly Funny, Deeply Human Tale of Death and Second Chances

The First Five Minutes begins the moment Trevor Dane dies. But instead of slipping away, time slows. He finds himself staring at a clock — counting down from five minutes. Five minutes to say the right thing. Change a choice. Fix a moment. Five minutes to make peace with the end… or start something new.

This story is comical, eerie, ambitious, and heartfelt — exploring life’s biggest regrets and strangest mysteries in the time it takes to pour a cup of coffee. In this liminal in-between, Trevor will face ex-girlfriends, lost dreams, childhood echoes, and maybe even the cosmos itself.

The First Five Minutes is not just a countdown. It’s a second chance — if you’re brave enough to take it.
