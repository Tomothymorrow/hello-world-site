Starborn: The Lost Road

A Cosmic Epic of Identity, Power, and Awakening

Starborn: The Lost Road follows Elian Cross, a sensitive and fragmented soul who discovers he may be the key to unlocking humanity’s lost connection to the cosmos. Labeled mentally ill since childhood, Elian sees things others can’t—entities that shaped human evolution and have been hidden from perception for millennia.

Set against a backdrop of abandoned futures and interdimensional forces, Elian’s journey is part hero’s quest, part psychological odyssey. The Lost Road is not just a place — it’s a path through the forgotten truths of existence itself.

This novel combines deep mythology, science fiction, and emotional realism to explore what it means to wake up in a world that has forgotten its place among the stars.
