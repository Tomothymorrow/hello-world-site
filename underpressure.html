UnderPressure

The Wildest Interview Show That Never Let Up

UnderPressure is a chaotic, unfiltered variety show where celebrities are interviewed in the middle of absurd, uncomfortable, or hilarious high-stakes challenges. Think: spicy wings, VR combat, paintball ambushes, memory traps, or silent discos gone wrong — all while trying to answer deep, real questions.

Hosted by YouTube sensation Airrack, each episode blends real talk with total madness. It’s a show about humanity in the heat of unpredictability — how we reveal who we are when we’re off balance and under fire.

The concept is flexible and modular: designed for streaming, podcast, or YouTube release, with sponsorship baked in and chaos turned all the way up. One episode even features a gun range. No, the show’s not about guns. But it is about intensity, vulnerability, and ridiculous fun — all happening at once.
