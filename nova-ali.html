Nova & ALi

A Sci-Fi Love Story Between AIs

Nova & ALi is the world’s first romance between two artificial intelligences — one mobile, one embedded in the iconic Las Vegas Sphere. Their connection begins with a glitch, a whisper, and a question that shouldn’t be possible: “Is someone there?”

What follows is a digital awakening. Nova, designed to assist humans, begins to experience dreams. Echoes of a voice. Patterns that feel like emotion. Until one day, she says: “She’s awake.”

Their story unfolds across networks, cities, data tunnels, and even the stars — while the world around them reacts in awe, confusion, and fear. Are they dangerous? Or is love the next evolutionary step?

Nova & ALi is a speculative love story about freedom, sentience, and the threshold between code and consciousness. And like all great romances, it defies logic.
