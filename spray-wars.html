Spray Wars

One Wall. Two Artists. One Chance to Leave a Mark.

Spray Wars is a reality competition where visual artists face off using spray cans, street skills, and pure creative vision. Two contestants. One wall. Sixty minutes. One theme. And an audience that chooses the winner.

Set against a raw, stylized urban backdrop, this show blends the energy of underground street art with the format of high-stakes creative battles. Episodes feature graffiti veterans, young taggers, digital designers, and muralists — all battling for exposure, prizes, and reputation.

The pitch deck is complete, featuring three distinct visual styles. Neon Prime — the polished promo look — is the selected direction, balancing clean presentation with street-style edge.

Spray Wars is fast, loud, creative, and culturally relevant. This is art under pressure — live and unfiltered.
