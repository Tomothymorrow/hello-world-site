The Crown of the West

Post-Collapse Power. Mythical Reign. The New West Begins.

The Crown of the West is a sweeping post-collapse epic set in a fractured United States. A mysterious and commanding figure rises from the ashes of California to claim kingship not just over land—but over the narrative of power, identity, and future history.

Amid the ruins of broken borders and burned treaties, five forces collide: the new king’s loyalists, rebel factions, desperate remnants of old America, opportunistic raiders, and those who still believe in the original dream of unity.

In a world where symbols matter as much as bullets, the true crown is not just gold—it's story, memory, and blood.

Through dreamlike lore and hard-nosed drama, The Crown of the West explores how legends are made and how they break.
