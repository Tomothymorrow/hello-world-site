Time Skizm

Fractured Time. Broken Truths. A Neon Memory War.

Time Skizm is a 1990s alt-reality sci-fi thriller drenched in neon, paranoia, and fractured identity. In a world that feels familiar but operates on laws of glitching timelines and invisible watchers, a man named Juno uncovers the ripple that split reality — and possibly his mind.

Haunted by memories he never lived and followed by agents that don’t exist, Juno navigates a dystopian dreamscape trying to stitch together who he is, and why he was chosen to remember the fracture no one else can see.

What begins as a mystery becomes a war — between those who create time and those who survive its collapse.

Finalized and fully edited, Time Skizm is Tomothy Morrow’s grittiest, darkest, and most cinematic work to date.
